# Meta HM Call Script and Scorecard

Use this in order. Score each answer:

- **2 = strong**
- **1 = mixed**
- **0 = weak / vague**

---

## Opening framing

> I’ve been ramping on the space and my current mental model is that this team sits at the feature platform layer for ranking — freshness, consistency, observability, latency, and cost. I’d love to understand how you think about the team’s charter and where this role would fit.

---

## Questions in order

### 1. Team charter

**Ask:** What is the core problem this team exists to solve?

**Strong:** Clear charter like feature freshness, sequence feature infra, consistency, ranking efficiency, launch/platform leverage.  
**Weak:** Buzzwords only.

**Score:** __ / 2

### 2. Your ownership

**Ask:** What exact area would I own in the first 6–12 months?

**Strong:** Named surface area with boundaries.  
**Weak:** “You’ll help across a few things.”

**Score:** __ / 2

### 3. Metrics / PSC

**Ask:** What are the top metrics this team is judged on, and what would mine likely be?

**Strong:** Freshness, p99, cost, adoption, launch speed, quality lift, reliability.  
**Weak:** Mainly unblocking others, no direct metrics.

**Score:** __ / 2

### 4. Team influence

**Ask:** If this team slipped by a quarter, which orgs or launches would feel it?

**Strong:** Named orgs, launches, concrete business/technical consequences.  
**Weak:** “Lots of teams use us.”

**Score:** __ / 2

### 5. Seat influence

**Ask:** Where does this role make decisions versus supporting partner teams?

**Strong:** Owns tradeoffs, standards, migrations, architecture.  
**Weak:** Mostly support and integration.

**Score:** __ / 2

### 6. Roadmap control

**Ask:** How much of the roadmap is proactive platform strategy versus incoming partner requests?

**Strong:** Team sets direction and drives standards.  
**Weak:** Mostly ticket intake from others.

**Score:** __ / 2

### 7. Cross-functional model

**Ask:** How do ML, Product, Data Science, and Core Infra each interact with this team?

**Strong:** Clear partner roles and clear team ownership.  
**Weak:** “We work with everyone.”

**Score:** __ / 2

### 8. Recognition / visibility

**Ask:** How do you make sure infra work gets recognized and not hidden behind partner teams?

**Strong:** Dashboards, reviews, docs, postmortems, upward visibility, explicit attribution.  
**Weak:** “People know who did it.”

**Score:** __ / 2

### 9. Growth path

**Ask:** What did a recent E5 project and a recent E6 promotion from this team look like?

**Strong:** Concrete examples with cross-team leadership and visible metrics.  
**Weak:** No examples, or only team-switch examples.

**Score:** __ / 2

### 10. Day-to-day reality

**Ask:** What does a normal week look like for this role?

**Strong:** Mix of build, design, debug, partner work, launches.  
**Weak:** Mostly firefighting, support, and meetings.

**Score:** __ / 2

### 11. Ramp / stack

**Ask:** How much is Python vs C++, and how much ML depth is needed on day one?

**Strong:** Clear split, realistic ramp expectations.  
**Weak:** Immediate deep expertise expected everywhere.

**Score:** __ / 2

### 12. Current pain points

**Ask:** What are the biggest technical pain points today?

**Strong:** Freshness, skew, latency, cost, reliability, adoption, serving bottlenecks.  
**Weak:** “Nothing major.”

**Score:** __ / 2

### 13. LLM usage

**Ask:** Are LLMs here mostly used for offline enrichment, online inference, or internal tooling?

**Strong:** Clear primary mode with concrete examples.  
**Weak:** “All of the above” with no detail.

**Score:** __ / 2

### 14. Manager support

**Ask:** How do you help people grow and get the right scope?

**Strong:** Intentional staffing, sponsorship, examples.  
**Weak:** “Good work gets noticed.”

**Score:** __ / 2
